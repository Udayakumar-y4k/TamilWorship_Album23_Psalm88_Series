வானங்கள் உம்முடைய அதிசயங்களைத் துதித்திடும் – கர்த்தரின் உண்மைக்கு துதி (சங்கீதம் 89).

Psalm 89 – Celebration of God’s faithfulness, covenant with David, and His unmatched power. The heavens declare His wonders, and His throne is established on righteousness and justice.

Closing Prayer
Lord Jesus Christ,
the heavens declare Your wonders and faithfulness.
Let our lives also praise You forever.
Establish Your covenant in us and make us faithful.
All glory to You alone.
